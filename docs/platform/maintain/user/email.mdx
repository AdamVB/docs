You control what email you receive from Mondoo. By default, Mondoo sends a weekly report on activity in your spaces. We also send news about:

- Our company and projects

- Product improvements and features

- Conferences we're attending and webinars we're hosting

## Unsubscribe from newsletters

1. In the [Mondoo Console](https://console.mondoo.com), select your user icon in the top-right corner.

2. Select the **Email Preferences** tab.

   ![Mondoo security settings](/img/platform/maintain/user/email.png)

3. Under **Newsletter Subscriptions**, select the toggle next to the type of message you no longer want to receive.

   Mondoo saves your changes automatically.

## Re-subscribe to newsletters

If you chose to stop receiving any of our newsletters but have changed your mind, contact us to re-subscribe:

- <a href="https://mondoo.link/slack">Mondoo community Slack channel</a>

- <a href="https://github.com/orgs/mondoohq/discussions">
    Mondoo GitHub community
  </a>

## Subscribe or unsubscribe to weekly space reports

1. In the [Mondoo Console](https://console.mondoo.com), select your user icon in the top-right corner.

2. Select the **Email Preferences** tab.

   ![Mondoo security settings](/img/platform/maintain/user/email.png)

3. Under **Email Notifications**, select the toggle next to **Send me weekly reports** to enable or disable weekly messages about the activity in all the spaces of which you're a member.

   Mondoo saves your changes automatically.
